## 5. SERVICES — DETAILED DESCRIPTIONS

### Lawn Maintenance (Residential & Commercial)
- Self-propelled and lightweight stand-on mowers only
- Best cut with no tire marks from heavy zero-turn mowers
- Year-round maintenance
- Tailored programs to enhance curb appeal and overall value

### Grounds Maintenance Programs
- Proactive, Florida-informed program
- Tailored to North Florida's heat, sandy soils, rainfall patterns, and storm season
- Follows UF/IFAS Florida-Friendly Landscaping principles: proper plant selection, efficient watering, appropriate fertilization, mulching, integrated pest management
- Keeps sites looking sharp in every season while protecting plant health, water resources, and property value

### Fertilization
- Professional fertilization ensuring lush, green lawns and thriving plant life
- Crews maintain GI-BMP credential from UF/IFAS/DEP
- FDACS Limited Commercial Fertilizer Applicator certification
- Calibrated, environmentally responsible applications

### Irrigation Management
- Programs built for North Florida's sandy soils, salty breezes, summer downpours, and long dry spells
- Science-based scheduling, precision hardware, strict local compliance
- Inspect, adjust, and perform minor repairs for maximum efficiency
- Helps save money and keep landscapes healthy

### Seasonal Flower Changeouts
- Ensures businesses are vibrant and colorful year-round
- Enhances exterior appeal

### Mulching
- Premium mulching services
- Retains moisture and prevents weed growth
- Fresh mulch or pine straw installation
- Improves soil health, regulates moisture, creates clean finished appearance

### Palm & Tree Pruning
- Promotes healthy growth and prevents hazards
- Best done late spring or early summer (before hurricane season)
- Only dead fronds below 9 to 3 o'clock line removed
- Avoids "hurricane haircuts" that increase vulnerability
- Green fronds preserved for photosynthesis and wind protection

### Sod Installation
- Instant and lasting transformation for commercial properties
- Process: remove old turf, grade for drainage, amend soil, roll for surface contact, seamless seams
- Dense coverage resists heavy foot traffic, seasonal stress, everyday use
- Mature root structure stabilizes soil, reduces erosion
- Can increase property value by up to 15%

### Aeration & Top Dressing
- Relieves soil compaction
- Balances pH
- Enriches soil
- Transforms commercial landscapes

### Tree & Shrub Planting
- Creates visually appealing and sustainable environments

### Plant Removal & Install
- Removes and replaces dead plant material
- Ensures fresh and attractive landscapes

### Fall & Spring Clean Ups
- Remove debris, trim overgrowth, edge beds, restore order
- Perfect for seasonal resets or preparing landscapes

### Pine Straw
- Alternative ground cover option
